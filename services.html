Our Services

Comprehensive mental health care for individuals, couples, families, and children. Find the support you need.

Therapy Services

We offer a range of therapeutic services tailored to your needs.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one support for your personal journey.

Work privately with a therapist to address your unique challenges, build coping skills, and achieve your personal goals. We treat a wide range of concerns including anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, and more.

  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Evidence-based approaches
  • In-person and telehealth options
Learn More

Couples Therapy

Strengthen your relationship together.

Whether you're navigating conflict, rebuilding trust, or simply want to deepen your connection, couples therapy provides tools and guidance for healthier relationships.

  • Gottman Method trained therapists
  • Communication skills building
  • Pre-marital counseling available
Learn More

Family Therapy

Healing happens together.

Family therapy helps family members communicate more effectively, resolve conflicts, and support each other through challenges. We work with families of all configurations.

  • Improved family communication
  • Conflict resolution strategies
  • Support through transitions
Learn More

Child & Teen Therapy

Age-appropriate support for young minds.

Our child and adolescent specialists use developmentally appropriate techniques, including play therapy, to help children and teens navigate emotional challenges.

  • Play therapy for younger children
  • Teen-specific approaches
  • Parent consultation included
Learn More

Group Therapy

Healing in community.

Group therapy offers the unique benefit of shared experience. Connect with others facing similar challenges while learning skills together in a supportive environment.

  • DBT skills groups
  • Support groups
  • Psychoeducational groups
Learn More

Medication Management

When therapy alone isn't enough.

Our psychiatric nurse practitioners provide comprehensive evaluation and medication management for mental health conditions, often in coordination with your therapy.

  • Psychiatric evaluation
  • Ongoing medication monitoring
  • Coordinated with therapy
Learn More

Flexible Treatment Options

In-Person Sessions

Meet with your therapist face-to-face at our comfortable Massapequa office. Many clients prefer the personal connection and dedicated space of in-person therapy.

  • Private, comfortable treatment rooms
  • No technical issues or connectivity concerns
  • Full body language communication
  • Convenient Long Island location

Telehealth Sessions

Access therapy from the comfort of your home via secure video platform. Telehealth offers flexibility and convenience while maintaining effectiveness.

  • No commute required
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Same quality care as in-person
  • HIPAA-compliant secure platform
