Meet Our Team

Compassionate, experienced clinicians dedicated to your mental health and wellbeing.

Finding the Right Fit

We understand that finding the right therapist is crucial. The therapeutic relationship is a significant predictor of positive outcomes, which is why we take matching seriously.

Our Matching Process

  1. You contact us with information about what you're looking for
  2. We suggest 1-2 clinicians who might be a good fit based on your needs
  3. You schedule an initial session to see if there's a connection
  4. If it's not the right fit, we're happy to help you find another option

Not sure who to choose? Contact us and we'll help guide you to the right clinician for your needs.
