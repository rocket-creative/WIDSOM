Individual Therapy

One-on-one support for your personal journey toward healing and growth.

What is Individual Therapy?

Individual therapy provides a safe, confidential space where you work one-on-one with a therapist to address your unique challenges, develop coping skills, and achieve your personal goals.

Whether you're struggling with anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship issues, or simply feeling stuck, individual therapy offers personalized support tailored to your specific needs and circumstances.

Our therapists use evidence-based approaches combined with genuine compassion to help you gain insight, build skills, and make meaningful changes in your life.

What We Treat

Our therapists have expertise across a wide range of concerns.

Anxiety & Stress

Generalized anxiety, panic attacks, social anxiety, phobias, and overwhelming stress

Depression & Mood

Major depression, persistent sadness, low motivation, mood swings

Trauma & PTSD

Past trauma, abuse, PTSD, difficult memories that continue to affect you

Life Transitions

Career changes, divorce, relocation, retirement, empty nest, major life shifts

Grief & Loss

Death of a loved one, divorce, job loss, or other significant losses

Relationship Issues

Communication problems, boundaries, self-worth, patterns in relationships

Self-Esteem

Low self-worth, negative self-talk, imposter syndrome, perfectionism

Personal Growth

Self-discovery, goal setting, improved wellbeing, living more authentically

Our Approach

We use evidence-based therapeutic approaches tailored to your specific needs:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) - Identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) - Building emotional regulation and distress tolerance skills
  • EMDR - Processing traumatic memories and reducing their emotional impact
  • Psychodynamic Therapy - Exploring how past experiences shape current patterns
  • Mindfulness-Based Approaches - Developing present-moment awareness and self-compassion
  • Solution-Focused Therapy - Building on your strengths to create positive change

Your therapist will work with you to determine the best approach based on your goals, preferences, and what's most likely to be effective for your situation.

What to Expect

Session Details

  • Duration: 45-50 minutes per session
  • Frequency: Weekly to start (adjusts as needed)
  • Format: In-person or telehealth
  • Location: Massapequa office or secure video

Your First Session

Your first session is an opportunity for your therapist to understand what brings you to therapy, learn about your history and current situation, and discuss your goals. You'll also have time to ask questions and determine if the therapist is a good fit. There's no pressure to share everything at once—you set the pace.

Insurance & Investment

We accept many major insurance plans. Contact us to verify your coverage.

Self-pay rates and sliding scale options are available for those who qualify. Don't let cost be a barrier—reach out to discuss your situation.
