About Pathways Within Wisdom

A compassionate mental health practice dedicated to guiding you toward healing, growth, and deeper self-understanding.

Our Story

Where It All Began

Pathways Within Wisdom was founded with a simple yet profound mission: to provide accessible, compassionate, and effective mental health care to the Long Island community.

Our founder, Rachel Lessard, LCSW, envisioned a practice where healing happens not just through talk therapy, but through honoring the interconnectedness of mind, body, and spirit. This holistic approach remains at the heart of everything we do.

What started as a small private practice has grown into a diverse team of skilled clinicians, each bringing unique expertise and a shared commitment to helping you find your own pathway within.

Why Pathways Within?

What sets us apart in mental health care.

Diverse Expertise

Our team includes therapists with specializations across many areas—anxiety, depression, trauma, ADHD, relationships, life transitions, and more. This means we can match you with a clinician who truly understands your specific needs.

Flexible Options

We offer in-person sessions at our Long Island locations as well as telehealth appointments, making it easier to fit therapy into your life.

Insurance-Friendly

We accept many major insurance plans and offer affordable self-pay rates. Don't let cost be a barrier to getting help.

Holistic Integration

As part of the Pathways Within family, we offer unique integration with wellness services—massage, acupuncture, energy work—for truly comprehensive care.

Medication Management Available

Our nurse practitioners provide psychiatric evaluation and medication management when needed, offering a complete treatment approach.

Community Connection

We're proud to be part of the Long Island community, serving families in Nassau County and beyond with care rooted in understanding local needs.
