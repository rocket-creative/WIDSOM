Our Therapeutic Approach

Evidence-based methods combined with compassionate, personalized care to support your unique healing journey.

Our Philosophy

At Pathways Within Wisdom, we believe that healing happens when you feel truly seen, heard, and supported. Our approach combines the best of evidence-based therapeutic methods with a deep respect for your individual experience.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all therapy. Instead, we meet you where you are and work collaboratively to develop a treatment plan tailored to your specific needs, goals, and preferences.

Our holistic perspective recognizes that mind, body, and spirit are interconnected. True healing often involves addressing all aspects of your wellbeing—not just managing symptoms.

Evidence-Based Methods

Proven therapeutic approaches tailored to your needs.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps you identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors that contribute to emotional distress. It's highly effective for anxiety, depression, and many other conditions.

Best for: Anxiety, depression, phobias, OCD, stress management

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

DBT combines cognitive techniques with mindfulness practices, helping you regulate emotions, improve relationships, and develop distress tolerance skills.

Best for: Emotional regulation, borderline personality, self-harm urges, intense emotions

EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing)

EMDR is a powerful therapy for processing traumatic memories and reducing their emotional impact. It helps your brain heal from trauma naturally.

Best for: PTSD, trauma, anxiety, phobias, disturbing memories

Psychodynamic Therapy

This approach explores how unconscious thoughts, past experiences, and relationship patterns influence your current life, leading to deeper self-understanding.

Best for: Self-exploration, relationship patterns, persistent issues, personal growth

Somatic Therapy

Somatic approaches recognize that trauma and stress are stored in the body. These techniques help release physical tension while healing emotional wounds.

Best for: Trauma, chronic stress, anxiety, body disconnection

Play Therapy

For children, play is their natural language. Play therapy allows young clients to express themselves, process emotions, and develop coping skills in age-appropriate ways.

Best for: Children experiencing anxiety, behavioral issues, trauma, family changes

Gottman Method Couples Therapy

Based on decades of research, the Gottman Method helps couples strengthen their relationship, improve communication, and resolve conflicts constructively.

Best for: Couples seeking to improve communication, rebuild trust, strengthen connection

Mindfulness-Based Approaches

Integrating mindfulness practices helps you develop present-moment awareness, reduce rumination, and build a more compassionate relationship with yourself.

Best for: Anxiety, depression, stress, emotional regulation, self-compassion

Session Structure

Individual Sessions

  • Duration: 45-50 minutes typically
  • Frequency: Weekly to start, adjusting as needed
  • Format: In-person or telehealth
  • Focus: Your unique goals and concerns

Couples & Family Sessions

  • Duration: 50-90 minutes depending on format
  • Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly
  • Format: In-person preferred, telehealth available
  • Focus: Relationship dynamics and communication
